ASUS Vivobook S 15 OLED S5507QA-MA081W Snapdragon X Elite X1E 16GB Memory 512GB SSD 15.6" OLED Display Copilot+ PC

ASUS Vivobook S 15 OLED S5507QA-MA081W Snapdragon X Elite X1E Copilot+ PC is a new generation laptop designed with advanced AI features. Commonly referred to as the Copilot+ PC, it includes a built-in Copilot button that enhances productivity and user experience. This Laptop comes with a stunning 15.6-inch OLED display with a 3K (2880 x 1620) 16:9 aspect ratio, 600nits HDR peak brightness, 1,000,000:1 contrast ratio, a 120Hz refresh rate, and 0.2ms response time. This display features VESA CERTIFIED Display HDR True Black 600, 1.07 billion colors, Glossy display, 70% less harmful blue light, TÜV Rheinland-certified, and SGS Eye Care Display. The OLED display provides a fuller viewing angle, a wider color range, and much faster refresh rates. This remarkable display makes it perfect for content creators, graphic designers, and anyone who values stunning visuals in their work or entertainment. It is powered by the latest Snapdragon X Elite X1E 78 100 Processor with Qualcomm Hexagon NPU 45TOPs NPU, which has 12 cores and 12 threads that deliver up to 3.4 GHz frequency with a massive 42MB cache. This high-frequency processor helps to process power-hungry applications with ease. So, this laptop can be the best option for people who use photo editing and video editing software and require powerful chipsets. This Asus laptop comes with the latest LPDDR5X 16GB Onboard memory. It has a 512GB M.2 NVMe PCIe 4.0 SSD. The NVMe SSD delivers the highest throughput and fastest response times for all types of enterprise workloads. This model of ASUS Vivobook S 15 OLED S5507QA-MA081W has integrated Qualcomm Adreno GPU, one of the world's most powerful graphics for Notebooks. For light gaming and content creation, this GPU is more than enough.




For long-term backup, it has 70WHrs, 3S1P, 3-cell Li-ion battery. You can enjoy your outdoor work or movie time without worrying about plugging the laptop into power. For better wireless connectivity it has the latest Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be) (Tri-band)2*2 and Bluetooth 5.4 Wireless Card. The Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be) (Tri-band)2*2 provides much faster wireless speeds and lower latency. This laptop comes with a Built-in Harman/Kardon (Mainstream) Audio system featuring Smart Amp Technology that provides a stunning audio experience. It has a variety of ports, including 2x USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-A, 2x USB 4.0 Gen 3 Type-C support display/power delivery, 1x HDMI 2.1 TMDS, 1x 3.5mm Combo Audio Jack, and a Micro SD card reader.



The FHD camera with IR function to support Windows Hello; With a privacy shutter is ideal for video conferences, and online classes. ASUS Vivobook S 15 OLED S5507QA-MA081W is packed with special features such as a Backlit Chiclet Keyboard 1-Zone RGB with Num-key With Copilot key to enable AI features instantly and a Trusted Platform Module (TPM) that provides security against threats like firmware and ransomware attacks. This laptop has US MIL-STD 810H military-grade standard certifications. Weighing at only 1.18 kg (2.60 lbs), this is an ultra-lightweight laptop. This Cool Silver-colored laptop is visually eye-pleasing. Box content includes Laptop, TYPE-C, 90W AC Adapter, 3-in-1 backpack, and user manuals. For peace of mind, Asus provides a 2-year brand warranty on laptops and a 1-year warranty on power adapters.
